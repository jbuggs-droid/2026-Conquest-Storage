Key Style Specifications for Standardization
Positioning:
Toplane: Centered to the map window, running the total horizontal width of the map window.
Middlelane: Centered to the map window, running the total horizontal width of the map window.
Border/Trim: 2px solid yellow/gold (rgba(255,230,140,.72)) on the top and bottom edges.
Background: A dark, linear-gradient (rgba(17,22,35,.97) to rgba(28,18,10,.95)) with a blur backdrop-filter.
Text Alignment:
Shipping Banners: Must be left-justified.
Trade Banners: Center-justified.
Interactivity: Includes a clickable "Continue" button; game action must pause until the banner is dismissed.
Behavior: Banners "slide" vertically to stay in their assigned lane (top or middle) regardless of browser scroll position.
